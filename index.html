Our Empanelments

Group Empanelment

To enable Banks and Financial Insitutions in taking informed lending decisions and in helping various Government Nodal bodies in taking decisions on issues such as land allotment, extending financial assistance to companies, and other related issues, LSI has been empanelled with most of the PSU Banks and various Government Nodal Bodies for conducting Techno Economic Viability Studies, valuation/Leaders Engineers, Preparation of Concession Agreements, Preparation of Detailed Project Reports (DPR’s) etc.

Empanelment with banks, financial institutions & PSU undertakings is for TEV study, lenders’ independent engineer, valuation and project appraisal, etc.
